Our Model

Our Farm Infrastructure as A Service Approach

The core focus of our business model is to create wealth across the
agricultural value chain by unlocking access to storage, markets, and
financing for farmers.

We ensure that farmers have have access to storage, financing, and
access to guaranteed markets; which creates an opportunity for
members of our WealthX cooperative to create wealth for themselves
through investments.

In addition, members of our cooperative are also able to save through
our monthly contribution plan, access affordable foodstuff, and earn
referral income when they bring others to join the WealthX cooperative.

Why Us?

When you register as a member of the WealthX Cooperative,
you automatically qualify to:
Earn annual membership dividends from all transactions done
on the platform, whether you invest or not, one year after
registering.
Earn referral income from bringing people to join the WealthX
cooperative.
Access verified and insured investment products with great
Returns on Investments.
Access affordable foodstuffs through the Stockup Service.
Save money through a monthly contribution plan on the
platform.
Auction your investments and withdraw your money at any time.
The WealthX Multipurpose Cooperative Society Limited is registered with the
mandate to cater for the collective needs of her members.

Our Corporate Objectives:

To build a community of people passionate about creating wealth
through investing in and financing projects in the agricultural and
allied sectors.
To share profits from investment projects with our members based
on their financial contributions.
To empower people at the bottom of the pyramid, especially
smallholder farmers in rural locations through access to storage,
financing, and markets.
To empower smallholder farmers with input financing and post
harvest loans.
To create alternative sources of income for Nigerians by enabling
them leverage our cooperative to create wealth.
We are creating social impact, reducing extreme poverty and hunger in rural
communities while guaranteeing food security.
